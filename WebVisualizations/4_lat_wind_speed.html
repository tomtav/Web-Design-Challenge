network_check Wind Speed

This visualization of the cities wind speeds by their latitude was used to determine if there was a correlation between latitude and wind speeds as you get closer to the equator.

In this diagram, you see there is a small positive correlation between wind speeds and latitude. Wind speeds increase as you move further away from the equator. The most common observations were speeds between 0 an 5 mph.
