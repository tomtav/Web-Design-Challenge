blur_on Latitude vs Humidity

This visualization of humidity and latitude was used to determine if there is any correlation between the percentage of humidity observed and latitude as you get closer to the equator.

To my surprise, there doesn't seem to be any significant correlation between humidity and latitude. The visualization shows a great variety of values at different latitudes.

However, you get a larger variety the closer you are to the equator (ranging between 0% and 100%).
