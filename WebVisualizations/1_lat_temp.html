wb_sunny Latitude vs Max Temperature

This visualization of the cities max temperature by their latitude was used to determine if the temperature get warmer as you get closer to the equator.

The further north or south from the equator you travel the location's temperature gets cooler. This means that the location's temperature has definite inverse correlation with it's latitude. As expected, the closer you are to the equator the warmer the weather is for those locations closer to 0 degrees latitude.
